Links and References

The site was completely compiled and written by slagcoin. Unique information from other sources is noted. All images were photographed or drawn by the author of this page except where noted otherwise.

Here is a list of links relevant to arcade joystick controllers and this site:

  • Sanwa: The most popular arcade joystick and button producer based in Japan.
  • Seimitsu: The second most popular arcade joystick and button producer based in Japan.
  • Happ: The most popular arcade joystick and button producer in the United States.
  • Suzo: Popular arcade joystick and button producer in Europe.
  • Industrias Lorenzo S.A.: Popular arcade joystick and button producer in Europe.
  • TaeYoung: Popular arcade joystick and button producer in Korea.
  • Ultimarc: Small producer of unique joysticks and PCBs in Japan.
  • Groovy Game Gear: Small producer of unique PCBs and mods and parts vendor in the United States.
  • Lizard Lick Amusements: Arcade parts vendor located in the United States.
  • Akihabara Shop: English-speaking arcade parts vendor located in Japan run by TheRealNeoGeo.
  • Gremlin Solutions: Arcade parts vendor located in the United Kingdom.
  • Arcade Shop: Arcade parts vendor located in Germany.
  • Hori: Varying quality joystick controller producer.
  • Arcade-in-a-Box: Custom quality controller producer.
  • Woodcraft: Woodworking retailer in the United States.
  • Rockler: Woodworking retailer in the United States.
  • Electronics123: Dirt cheap specialty electronics vendor in the United States.
  • Digi-Key: Vendor of specialty electronics in many areas of the world.
  • McMaster-Carr: Vendor of many parts in the United States.
  • Harbor Freight: Dirt cheap tools vendor in the United States.
  • Byrdo: High quality individual joystick controller maker.
  • Arcade Controls: Extensive arcade building information site.
  • Shoryuken: Extensive fighting games related site with lots of hardware input.
  • kowal: Polish arcade joystick information site with many joystick reviews.
  • Joystick Vault: Gallery-based site with a ton of great images of custom joysticks.
  • SD Tekken: Site by Markman with deep controller and converter reviews.
  • Toodles: Fan of joysticks and modification who offers some PCB-related products.
